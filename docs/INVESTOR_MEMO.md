## The Problem: A Massive Industry Still Running on Paper

Home based care is one of the fastest growing sectors in U.S. healthcare, sitting inside a $343B digital health market. Yet the 35,000 licensed home care agencies that deliver this care still rely on manual, error prone processes to onboard clients and generate contracts.

Here is what a typical intake looks like today:

1. A care coordinator visits a client's home and takes handwritten notes
2. Back at the office, they spend hours manually typing up the assessment
3. An admin re-enters the data into billing software
4. Someone else drafts a contract in Word, triple checking rates and service codes
5. The contract is emailed, printed, signed, scanned, and filed

Agencies lose 40 to 60% of staff time to this paperwork. It takes days to go from first visit to signed contract, clients are lost to faster competitors, and missed billables cost agencies thousands every month while adding compliance risk.

---

## The Solution: The Visit Itself Becomes the Paperwork

PalmCare AI collapses the entire intake to contract workflow into a single action: **record the conversation**.

A care coordinator taps record on their phone, conducts the assessment naturally, and PalmCare AI handles everything else:

- **Transcribes** the conversation with speaker identification using Deepgram Nova-3
- **Extracts** care needs, service hours, and billable items using Claude Sonnet 4.6
- **Generates** SOAP notes and a complete, state compliant service contract
- **Exports** to PDF or DOCX, ready to send or print

One visit. Same day paperwork. No typing, no re-entry, no back and forth.
