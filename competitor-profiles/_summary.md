## How they get traffic (inferred)

1. **Keyword-led titles** — category term first ("AI Scribe for Home Health"), brand last.
2. **Quantified proof everywhere** — % time saved, $ productivity, accuracy, go-live time, ROI calculators. Stats are the top GEO/AEO citation booster.
3. **On-page comparison tables** (Scribble: vs Dictation / Consumer LLMs / Manual).
4. **Named testimonials** (role + organization) → E-E-A-T.
5. **FAQ sections** (FAQ schema), problem-agitation content, resource libraries, PR.
6. All crowd the **home-health/OASIS** keyword space.

## Our edge & gaps closed (this round)

- **Lane:** Own **home _care_** (non-medical) + **service contract / care plan / assessment** keywords — uncontested by the scribes; none produce contracts.
