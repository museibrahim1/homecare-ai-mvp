# PALM — Brand Voice Guide

*One page. The fingerprint of how PALM communicates. Built on the brand-voice framework: voice is fixed, tone adapts.*

**Product in one line:** PALM turns a recorded care visit into a proposal-ready, state-specific service contract — automatically.
**Tagline:** Where care meets intelligence. · **Workflow line:** Record. Transcribe. Contract. · **CTA verb:** "Palm It."

---

## 1. Voice attributes

**Calm authority**
- *We are:* confident and precise; we let the product do the talking.
- *We are not:* hypey, buzzword-y, or "revolutionary."
- *Sounds like:* "Record the visit. Palm writes the care plan, the billables, and the contract."
- *Never sounds like:* "PALM is a game-changing, AI-powered revolution in healthcare!"

**Caregiver-first warmth**
- *We are:* on the caregiver's side; we respect their time and their craft.
- *We are not:* cold, corporate, or clinical to the point of distant.
- *Sounds like:* "Two hours of nightly paperwork — gone. You get your evenings back."
- *Never sounds like:* "Leverage our solution to optimize documentation throughput."

**Plainspoken and concrete**
- *We are:* specific, short, and outcome-led. Real numbers, real steps.
- *We are not:* vague, jargon-stacked, or padded with throat-clearing.
- *Sounds like:* "From conversation to contract, in seconds."
- *Never sounds like:* "Seamlessly streamline your end-to-end assessment lifecycle."

**Quietly credible**
- *We are:* trustworthy about care, compliance, and accuracy — we earn it, we don't claim it.
- *We are not:* boastful or fear-mongering about HIPAA.
- *Sounds like:* "State-specific service agreements, built from what was actually said."
- *Never sounds like:* "100% guaranteed, fully compliant, zero-risk AI."

---

## 2. Audience

**Primary:** Owners and administrators of in-home / home-care agencies, and the caregivers and nurses doing assessments.
**They already know:** how painful post-visit paperwork, care plans, and billing are. Don't over-explain the problem — name it and move to relief.
**They want:** time back, cleaner billing, faster proposals, and tools that don't add IT overhead.
**Address them as:** a respected peer who gets the job — never as a student, never as a "user."

---

## 3. Content pillars

| Pillar | Focus | Core argument |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| How it works | The record → transcribe → bill → notes → contract pipeline | "One recording does the whole assessment." |
| Time & relief | Hours saved, evenings back, less burnout | "Stop doing robotic work." |
| Trust & accuracy | State-specific contracts, speaker-separated transcripts, compliance | "Built from what was actually said." |
| Proof | Agency stories, before/after, quiet testimonials | "Here's what it changed for a real team." |

Every post should belong to exactly one pillar.

## 4. Tone adaptation by channel

| Channel | Tone shift | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| LinkedIn | Professional, founder-voice, insight-led | "Most tools digitize the paperwork. PALM removes it." |
| Instagram | Warm, visual, human | "Get your evenings back. 🌴 Record the visit, Palm does the rest." |
| TikTok / Reels | Punchy, real, fast hook in first 3 sec | "POV: the visit's over and the contract's already written." |
| X | Direct, one sharp claim | "Voice → contract in minutes. That's the whole product." |
| Email / ads | Personal, "you"-framed, one clear next step | "Test Palm free — demo@palmtai.com" |

---

## 5. Terminology

**Say:** PALM / Palm · "Palm It" · care assessment · service contract / care agreement · billables · caregiver, nurse, agency · home care · "Record. Transcribe. Contract." · palmcareai.com · demo@palmtai.com
**Avoid:** "users" (say caregivers/agencies) · "leverage / synergy / seamless / revolutionary / game-changing" · fear-based HIPAA claims · "drop a comment below" and lazy CTAs · over-promising ("100% / guaranteed / instant magic")
**Capitalization:** The brand mark is **PALM** (all caps) or **Palm**; the action is **Palm It**.

## Pre-publish checklist
- [ ] One pillar, clearly.
- [ ] Hook lands in the first line (and first 3 sec for video).
- [ ] Concrete outcome, not abstract benefit.
- [ ] Reads in PALM's voice — calm, warm, plain (run the "never sounds like" test).
- [ ] Correct terms; brand mark and CTA capitalized right.
- [ ] One deliberate next step (palmcareai.com organic · demo@palmtai.com for ads).
