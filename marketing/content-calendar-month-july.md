# PALM Monthly Content Calendar (Jul 13 to Aug 9, 2026)

> **v4, the polished rebuild.** Every creative below is designed in-house from PALM's real
> brand assets: the actual hand logo, the actual app screenshots, the site typeface (Inter),
> and the app's teal. No AI-generated images, no invented logos, no stock-photo look, and
> no AI-slop punctuation (no em-dashes, no throwaway slashes).
>
> Built with the creative studio at `marketing/creative-studio/` (HTML and CSS templates
> rendered to pixel-perfect PNG). Any card can be edited and re-rendered in seconds:
> `node marketing/creative-studio/render.js`
>
> Formats follow current platform data: 4:5 (1080x1350) images for Meta feed,
> a 7-page 1200x1200 PDF document carousel for LinkedIn (the highest-engagement
> LinkedIn format in 2026, about 7% average engagement versus 3.25% for link posts).
>
> **LinkedIn rule (2026): keep the post body link-free and value-dense.** LinkedIn now
> throttles reach for external links in the body, and increasingly for first-comment links
> too. Our approach: full value in the post, the signup link in the first comment as a
> low-key CTA, and the URL kept off the image itself. Meta captions carry the link directly;
> IG uses link in bio.
>
> Cadence: Meta (FB and IG) Mon, Wed, Fri. LinkedIn Tue, Thu. 20 posts total.
> Approval-first: nothing posts until Muse confirms.
