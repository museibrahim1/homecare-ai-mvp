## Design Notes:

### Color Palette:
- Primary: Teal (#0d9488)
- Secondary: White (#ffffff)
- Accent: Light teal (#14b8a6)
- Text: Dark gray (#374151)

### Typography:
- Headers: Bold, 28-36px
- Body text: Regular, 16-18px
- CTA buttons: Semi-bold, 18px
- Small text: Regular, 14px

### Image Requirements:
- High-quality healthcare stock photos
- Clean, modern UI mockups
- Professional healthcare settings
- Diverse representation of caregivers and patients

### Platform Specifications:
- Instagram Square: 1080x1080px
- Instagram Story: 1080x1920px
- LinkedIn Post: 1200x628px
- Twitter/X: 1200x675px
- Facebook: 1200x630px
