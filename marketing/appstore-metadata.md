## Description

PalmCare AI transforms home care assessments with AI-powered voice recording and real-time transcription. Record client visits, generate clinical notes, create service contracts, and manage your caseload — all from your phone.

**Real-Time Voice Transcription**
Record assessments with automatic speech-to-text powered by Deepgram Nova-3. Speaker identification separates caregiver and client dialogue in real time, with medical terminology highlighted for quick review.

**AI-Generated Clinical Notes**
Every recorded assessment is automatically processed into structured clinical documentation including ADL/IADL scores, safety assessments, mental health evaluations, and care plan recommendations.

**Smart Contract Generation**
PalmCare AI analyzes assessment data to generate customized home care service agreements with appropriate services, rates, and schedules based on the client's needs and payer type (Medicaid, Medicare, Private Pay).

**Complete Client Management**
Maintain detailed client profiles with medical history, medications, allergies, emergency contacts, insurance information, and care plans. Track visit history and monitor client progress over time.

**Built for HIPAA Compliance**
End-to-end encryption, secure authentication with Face ID/Touch ID, automatic inactivity lock, and on-device data protection. Your client data stays secure.

**Key Features:**
• Live voice recording with real-time transcription
• AI-powered speaker identification (caregiver vs. client)
• Automated clinical assessment notes
• Smart service contract generation
• Client management with full medical profiles
• Visit tracking with processing pipeline
• Calendar scheduling
• Document management
• Dark mode support
• Background recording for long assessments
• HIPAA-compliant security architecture

PalmCare AI is designed for home care agencies, independent caregivers, nursing professionals, and care coordinators who need efficient, accurate documentation without the paperwork.
