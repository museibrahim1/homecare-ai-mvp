================================================================================
  JANUARY — Incorporation + Foundation
  Goal: Remove legal & execution blockers
================================================================================

  WEEK OF JAN 5–11
  ─────────────────
  Operations / Legal
    • Finalize incorporation
    • Open bank account
    • Set up accounting (QuickBooks or equivalent)
    • Issue founder equity docs

  Logistics
    • Sign NDA with manufacturer
    • Send full technical package + scope
    • Confirm manufacturer timeline
    • Confirm tooling vs pilot run requirements
    • Lock communication cadence (weekly call)

  Marketing
    • Secure brand handles + domain
    • Draft positioning doc (1–2 pages)

  WEEK OF JAN 12–18
  ──────────────────
  Operations / Legal
    • File provisional patent (or finalize draft)
    • Create internal project tracker (Notion / ClickUp)

  Logistics
    • Review DFM feedback
    • Identify secondary suppliers (backup)

  Marketing
    • Define ICP (who buys first)
    • Outline launch narrative (problem → solution)

  WEEK OF JAN 19–25
  ──────────────────
  Operations / Legal
    • Advisor outreach (manufacturing, brand, legal)
    • Build investor update template

  Logistics
    • Confirm BOM cost targets
    • Align on pilot quantities

  Marketing
    • Start content capture (photos, sketches, story)
    • Build email waitlist landing page (simple)
